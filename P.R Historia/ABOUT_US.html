ABOUT ME

Yandel Colón Rivera

Born in Fajardo, Puerto Rico, Yandel Colón currently studies in Interfajardo. He is an up and coming web developer that seeks for the improvement of educational resources. Through the use of technology he hopes to create an easy transition of textbooks to technological sources. The lack of self-actualization of important information and administration of the government has led him to create a space for historical events to shine. I have created multiple reliable resources to increase accessibility.

Skills

  • Proficiency in Front End languages such as HTML, CSS and Javascript.
  • Bilingual (English and Spanish)
  • Designing and prototyping visual frameworks through Figma.

What is the objective of this project?

  • Education

    Promote and foment a strong cultural identity. Through the use of this channel we hope to create well-formed cultural identity by seeing the stages of its evolution.

  • Interactivity

    Engaging way for students to learn, thus,

  • Efficiency

    A manner in which teachers can educate history to the children faster.

  • Accessibility

    Easy access of information for all ages. Knowledge is for all and should not be restricted regardless of the stage of life someone is in.

  • Awareness

    Awareness and understanding behind our identities, creating a unified form of self.

  • Cultural Pride

    Showcase the range of expression of our culture to the younger generation. Demonstrating the hardship, the struggle, the success and the love that goes through us all as a nation.

  • Preservation

    To preserve the knowledge of our ancestors in the mind of the young. To never forget who we are and what we represent, what we are and what will be.

  • Truthfulness

    Demonstrate a source faithful to the truth. No deviation from the truth, no need for lies, no need to hide what is ours to know.

  • Acknowledge

    Bring to light those unrepresented by the media. By doing so we bring honor to those who paved the way for our people.

  • Self-actualization

    A program that will actualize itself with the current understanding of our history.

What we hope to accomplish.

Create a safe environment for learning

Receive widespread recognition in order to garner a following.

Receive follow-up on educational projects.

Counteract the lack of education/educational deficiencies in the country.

An effective management of the learning tools.
