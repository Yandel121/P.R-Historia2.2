Who are you?

I am a developer who seeks to ease the transition between traditional learning methods to modernize ways while providing a platform both engaging and effective for teachers and students alike. I don't seek to disregard older methods but rather enhance their effectiveness, keeping in mind the current research regarding how humans behave and learn.

What is your method of education?

Our method of education is called A.C.E.S

Aligning fun with education
Collaboration among peers
Entertainment through modern media
Singular-multiple style learning

What is this type of learning meant to promote?

Group-focused learning

Take into account the various learning styles.

Modernize how we teach.

Integrate several styles of learning.

Phase 1: Lecture

Our website offers a collection of reputable, verified, and certified content to choose from. We always try to maintain at the top of the selection the most relevant and up-to-date content while keeping the dubious and more subjective topics at the bottom. This lets the reader find the content of their choice regardless of authenticity. If they decide to read outdated content, this will be highlighted/notified to the reader to avoid confusion and ultimately avoid misinformation.

Phase 2: Games

Methodology of learning is meant to provide auditory and visual learning through engaging, entertaining content. Research has shown that when we gamify educational sources, students tend to have better retention and overall knowledge of the subject. Such a powerful tool will lead to further development in favor of the student.

Phase 3: Review

This methodology of learning is focused on the content a child needs to reinforce. For example, if in the reading phase the student didn't show certain understanding over the topic, it will display various exercises to break down the content into a way the student may best understand the subject.

Phase 4: Quizzes

The final phase is to prove the knowledge of the student over the material given to them. If the student is subjected to these methods and still struggles, they can revisit some of the other steps.
